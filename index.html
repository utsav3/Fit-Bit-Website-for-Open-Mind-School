About:
Bay Area Specialfit is an inclusive fitness program for children, teens, and adults in the Bay Area community. Programs have a strong emphasis on principles of human movement, human capacity, and physical literacy while scaffolding support for individual differences. Individual and group classes aim to improve the quality of life for individuals through the development of relationships, the implementation of functional fitness, and integration into a welcoming and inclusive gym environment. Our mission is to expand the current paradigm focused primarily on traditional occupational and physical therapy interventions and adapted PE; to include fitness training and regular workouts. The goal is to create a program that will greatly enhance longevity and elevate the quality of physical activity throughout all stages of a person’s lifetime.By emphasizing fitness as a whole, we hope to support more positive, confident, and able-bodied individuals.
